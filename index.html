D Y Patil Hospital or D.Y.P.H.R.C.

D Y Patil Hospital or D.Y.P.H.R.C. is a charitable hospital in Navi Mumbai, India. It was founded in 2004 by Shri Dnyandeo Yashwantrao Patil (Ex-Governor of Tripura, West Bengal and Bihar). It has 1500 beds dedicated to charity, a 300-bed ICU facility (the largest in Navi Mumbai).

Campus Life

school auditorium

The Auditorium of the DY Patil School of Medicine, located centrally on the 6th floor of the college building forms an integral and salient part of the college. It is a well spaced room that can seat around 300 people

school library

Library was established in the year 2000. It has come a long way since then. The ambience of library provides pleasant and quiet atmosphere for studies. Self-development and learning starts from a student's mind

school stadium

The DY Patil Stadium also known as DY Patil Sports Stadium is a cricket and football stadium at D. Y. Patil campus at Nerul in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.The stadium also has hosted 2017 FIFA U-17 World Cup matches
